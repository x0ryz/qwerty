01. THE MISSION

We believe that the tools you use define the work you create. In an era of disposable plastic and planned obsolescence, QWERTY stands for permanence. We build input devices that outlast the computers they control.

02. THE AESTHETIC

We reject the "gamer" aesthetic. No RGB rainbows. No aggressive angular plastic. Our design language is rooted in the brutalist architecture of the 1970s and the utilitarian industrial design of early computing terminals. Strict. Functional. Timeless.

03. THE BUILD

  • Milled 6063 Aluminum Chassis
  • Heavy gauge steel backplates
  • Double-shot PBT Keycaps (1.5mm)
  • Hand-lubed stabilizers
  • VIA/QMK Programmable PCBs
  • Sound dampening Poron foam kits
